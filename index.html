Daffodil Computers

Daffodil Computers was established in 1990 as a proprietorship concern of Mr. Md. Sabur Khan, the former president of Dhaka Chamber of Commerce and Industries (DCCI) and Bangladesh Computer Somity (BCS). In 1998 the Company was incorporated as a Private Limited Company and named Daffodil Computers Limited (DCL). In 2003 the company become the 1st Public Listed Company as a Purely IT based Company in Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) and in 2006 also listed in Chittagong Stock Exchnage (CSE). DCL has emerged as the largest computer assembling, marketing and selling firms of Bangladesh.
